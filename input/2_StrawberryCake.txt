2. Strawberry Cake by Nacer Bouchekhima [2019-11-10]
A delicious recipe for strawberry.
Ingredients:
200 unsalted butter
175g caster sugar
3 eggs
115g self-raising flour
1 fresh strawberry

Method:
1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C.
2. Beat together butter and sugar.
3. Mix in eggs, ont at a time.
4. Sift in flour and add lemon zest.

6. Bake for 40-55 minutes.
